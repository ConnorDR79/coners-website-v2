The Pinelodge Pinecones

About Our Club

The Pinelodge Pinecones are an ancient unoffial Australian Rules Football Club originating in the land of Pinelodge. Our players are known for their unmatched physical prowess and include such household names as Angelo "Stud" Flaminiano, Brendan "The Dook" Meadows and Maz "Maz" Mazurak.

Brief History of the Coners

Originating around the year of 2016, the Pinelodge Pinecones began when two young undiscovered geniuses - Connor Randall and Brendan Meadows - decided to take fate into their own hands. Sick of being the best footballers to ever grace the Dookie United Football Club, our 2 heroes decided to found their own club in order to find fellow individuals who matched their insofar unequaled football skill and general handsomeness. And so the Pinelodge Pinecones came into existence.....
